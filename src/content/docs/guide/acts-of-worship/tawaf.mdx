**Tawaf** is the act of circumambulating the **Ka’bah** seven times in a counterclockwise direction. It is a fundamental pillar of **Hajj** and **‘Umrah**, and must be performed with due care, purity, and sincerity.

---

## Types of Tawaf

- **Tawaf al-Qudum** – Arrival Tawaf (optional for non-residents of Makkah)
- **Tawaf al-‘Umrah** – Mandatory part of ‘Umrah
- **Tawaf al-Ifadah** – **A pillar (rukn)** of Hajj, performed after standing in Arafah
- **Tawaf al-Wada’** – Farewell Tawaf (wājib on all except menstruating women)
- **Tawaf al-Tatawwu’** – Voluntary circumambulation at any time

## Conditions for Valid Tawaf

- **Niyyah (intention)** must be made.
- **Wudū is required.**  
  > 🔗 [IslamQA: Wudū is a condition for Tawaf](https://islamqa.info/en/answers/34695)
- **Covering the ‘awrah** is mandatory.
- Women must be free of **menstruation or post-natal bleeding**.  
  > 🔗 [IslamWeb: Tawaf not valid during menstruation](https://www.islamweb.net/en/fatwa/84288)

## Corners of the Ka‘bah

1. **Black Stone (Ḥajar al-Aswad)**:
   - Say: **بِسْمِ ٱللّٰهِ وَٱللّٰهُ أَكْبَرُ**  
     _Bismillāhi wallāhu akbar_
   - Optionally:  
     اللَّهُمَّ إِيمَانًا بِكَ وَتَصْدِيقًا بِكِتَابِكَ وَوَفَاءً بِعَهْدِكَ وَاتِّبَاعًا لِسُنَّةِ نَبِيِّكَ مُحَمَّدٍ ﷺ  
     (_Allāhumma īmānan bika..._)
   - Kiss, touch, or point (in that order of priority).  
   > 🔗 [IslamQA](https://islamqa.info/en/answers/40184)

2. **Yemeni Corner (al-Rukn al-Yamānī)**:
   - Touch only — **no kissing or gesturing**.
   - Between Yemeni and Black Stone, say:

<Citation
  arabic="رَبَّنَا آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً، وَفِي الْآخِرَةِ حَسَنَةً، وَقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ"
  transliteration="Rabbana ātinā fī al-dunyā ḥasanah wa fī al-ākhirati ḥasanah wa qinā ʿadhāba al-nār."
  translation="Our Lord, give us good in this world and good in the Hereafter, and save us from the punishment of the Fire."
  source="Sunan Abi Dawud 1892"
  sourceLink="https://sunnah.com/abudawud:1892"
/>

3. **Shāmī and ‘Irāqī Corners** – No action is required.

---

## What If You Cannot Reach the Black Stone?

- Face it from afar.
- Raise your right hand.
- Say: **بِسْمِ ٱللّٰهِ وَٱللّٰهُ أَكْبَرُ**  
> 🔗 [IslamQA: Gesturing to the Black Stone](https://islamqa.info/en/answers/109254)

---

## How to Perform Tawaf (Step-by-Step)

1. Begin at the **Black Stone**.
   - Face it, raise your hand (or kiss or touch), and say:  
     **بِسْمِ ٱللّٰهِ وَٱللّٰهُ أَكْبَرُ**  
     _Bismillāhi wallāhu akbar_

2. Perform **7 rounds counter-clockwise**.
   - Each round starts and ends at the Black Stone.
   - **Men**: Brisk walking (raml) in first 3 rounds.
   - **Women**: Walk normally.

3. Engage in **dhikr, du‘ā**, and **Qur’an**.
   - No fixed du‘ā required between corners.
   - Example dhikr:

<Citation
  arabic="سُبْحَانَ اللهِ، وَالْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ، وَلَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ، وَاللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ"
  transliteration="SubḥānAllāh, wal-ḥamdu lillāh, wa lā ilāha illallāh, wallāhu akbar."
  translation="Glory be to Allah, all praise is due to Allah, there is no god but Allah, and Allah is the Greatest."
  source="General dhikr recommended in all worship"
  sourceLink="https://islamqa.info/en/answers/21474"
/>

4. Complete 7 full rounds. Do **not** stop mid-circuit.

---

## After Tawaf

1. **Pray 2 Rak‘ahs behind Maqam Ibrāhīm**, if possible:

> **وَاتَّخِذُوا مِن مَّقَامِ إِبْرَاهِيمَ مُصَلًّى**  
> _“And take the Maqam of Ibrāhīm as a place of prayer.”_  
> (Qur’an 2:125)

> 🔗 [IslamQA](https://islamqa.info/en/answers/106572)

   - If crowded, pray anywhere in the Haram.
   - Recommended: Surah al-Kāfirūn + al-Ikhlāṣ.

2. **Drink Zamzam water**:

<Citation
  arabic="مَاءُ زَمْزَمَ لِمَا شُرِبَ لَهُ"
  transliteration="Mā’u Zamzam limā shuriba lah."
  translation="The water of Zamzam is for whatever it is drunk for."
  source="Sunan Ibn Mājah 3062"
  sourceLink="https://sunnah.com/ibnmajah:3062"
/>

   - Make personal du‘ā while drinking.
   - Recommended:

<Citation
  arabic="اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ عِلْمًا نَافِعًا، وَرِزْقًا وَاسِعًا، وَشِفَاءً مِنْ كُلِّ دَاءٍ"
  transliteration="Allāhumma innī as’aluka ʿilman nāfiʿan, wa rizqan wāsiʿan, wa shifā’an min kulli dā’."

## Tawaf al-Ifadah

- Also called **Tawaf al-Ziyārah**
- It is a **pillar of Hajj** — Hajj is **not valid** without it
- Performed after standing at Arafah, from **10th Dhul Hijjah** onward
- Same rules as all other Tawaf

> 🔗 [IslamQA: Ruling on Tawaf al-Ifadah](https://islamqa.info/en/answers/109325)

---

## Rules for Women

- Same steps apply.
- No raml (brisk walk)
- No shoulder exposure
- Must delay Tawaf if menstruating  
  > 🔗 [IslamQA](https://islamqa.info/en/answers/14009)

---

## Common Mistakes

- Starting from the wrong point
- Incomplete or over rounds
- Breaking wudū
- Selfies and distractions  
  > 🔗 [IslamWeb](https://www.islamweb.net/en/fatwa/83871)

---

## Expiation if Tawaf is Invalid

- Missing a required Tawaf can invalidate Hajj or Umrah
- May require **dam** (sacrificial penalty)
- Always consult a scholar if uncertain  
  > 🔗 [IslamQA](https://islamqa.info/en/answers/12165)
